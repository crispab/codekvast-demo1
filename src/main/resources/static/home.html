This app is instrumented with Codekvast Agent

I guess that you want to see what data Codekvast Agent is collecting?

Normally, you do that by issuing heroku addons:open codekvast from your Heroku app's development workspace (or from the Heroku dashboard).

That will do a Single-Sign On (SSO) to Codekvast Warehouse with your Heroku credentials, allowing you to browse the data for the instrumented app.

For demonstration purposes, this sample app is able to perform the same SSO as Heroku. This enables you to immediately kick the tyres of Codekvast without further ado.

Also for demonstration purposes, this sample app uses a special demo plan, which makes collected data available much faster than for a normal client.
